❓Frequently Asked Questions

1. How does the lay-by plan work?

You choose your payment period (2 to 5 months), make a first deposit, and we reserve the device for you. Once it’s fully paid, we deliver it.

2. When is my device reserved?

Your item is only reserved after we receive your first installment.

3. Do you offer cash on delivery?

No, for safety reasons we don’t offer cash on delivery. All payments are made upfront or through our lay-by plan.

4. Is there interest or credit checks?

No. All payment plans are 100% interest-free, and we do not perform any credit checks.

5. What delivery options do you offer?

We offer free delivery in Cape Town and nationwide PostNet-to-PostNet delivery.

6. Do your devices come with a warranty?

  • New devices: 12-month warranty
  • Pre-owned devices: 3-month warranty

7. Can I upgrade my device before finishing my payment plan?

Yes, upgrading is available without extending your current payment plan.

8. What if I miss a payment?

We allow a grace period, but prolonged non-payment may lead to cancellation of the lay-by and loss of your deposit. Please communicate with us to avoid issues.

9. Can I pay off early?

Yes, you can settle your payment early at any time without penalties.

10. How do I contact you?

You can reach us via WhatsApp, email, or the Contact page on our website.

📜 Terms & Conditions

Warranty Policy

  • All used devices come with a 3-month limited warranty covering manufacturing defects and hardware malfunctions.
  • All brand new devices include a 12-month warranty as per manufacturer guidelines.
  • Warranty excludes:
    • Accidental damage (including water or impact damage)
    • Software tampering or unauthorized repairs
    • Cosmetic wear and tear

Returns & Exchanges

  • Requests must be made within 7 days of purchase.
  • Item must be in original condition with all accessories included.
  • Refunds (if applicable) are subject to inspection and approval.

Lay-By Policy

  • Custom lay-by plans range from 2 to 5 months.
  • Reservation starts once the first installment is received.
  • Item is released after final payment is completed.
  • If payments are missed, SmartLoom may cancel the lay-by and retain a cancellation fee.

Trading & Buying Devices

  • Devices are accepted based on condition, market demand, and authenticity.
  • Valid ID is required for all trades and sales.

Delivery

  • Nationwide PostNet-to-PostNet delivery available.
  • Free delivery for customers within Cape Town.
  • Tracking details are shared once the item is dispatched.
SmartLoom is not responsible for courier delays after dispatch.
